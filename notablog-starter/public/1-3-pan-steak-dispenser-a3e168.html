1/3 Pan Steak Dispenser

Description

This third pan dispenser supports Chipotle steak.

This dispenser has 6 parts:

Hyphen Hint: While dispenser bodies may look similar, the chicken and steak dispenser bodies have a shorter slide to accommodate product specs.

Dispenser Assembly

  1. Gather the 6 components of the dispenser.
  2. Place the dispenser on a stable surface. The labeled front of the dispenser should be facing toward you.
  3. Lay the dispenser down. Line up the top of the slide cover with the bottom of the hopper slide. Make sure the walls of the slide cover are hugging the exterior of the dispenser slide. Gently slide the cover upward until the slide tab audibly clips into place on the dispenser body.
  4. Install horizontal augers
    1. Horizontal auger 1 will be installed in the position closest to you. Slide the auger into the dispenser body until the base of the auger meets with anchor point 1. With slight pressure, gently turn the auger until it slides onto the anchor point.
    2. Horizontal Auger 2 will be installed in the position farthest from you. Slide the auger into the dispenser body until the base of the auger meets with anchor point 2. With slight pressure, gently turn the auger until it slides onto the anchor point.
  1. Install Vertical Augers: Auger 1 will be installed in the position closest to you. Locate the vertical anchor point inside of the auger well on the bottom left of the hopper body. Hold the auger by the flat top and gently slide the base toward the anchor point closest to you inside the dispensing area. With slight pressure, gently turn the auger until it slides onto the anchor point. Repeat for auger 2.

Hyphen Hint: The Front of the dispenser may change depending on where the dispenser is within the makeline. Instructions can be followed similarly as long as the labeled Front side is facing the operator.

Completed Assembly

The dispenser is now ready to receive food, and can be filled off or on the makeline.

Fill Dispenser with Chicken

  1. With the front of the dispenser facing you, line up a third pan of steak edge to edge with the side of the dispenser and gently empty contents into the dispenser, to the right of the interior wall. Use a solid spoon to direct the product from the pan into the dispenser.
  2. DO NOT load food into the auger well
  3. Dispenser can be filled similarly on or off the makeline.

Install Dispenser on the Makeline

  1. Install dispenser on make line. With the front of the dispenser facing towards you, grasp the dispenser at the top of the left and right side and lift it up and into the makeline.
  2. Place the dispenser onto the gear and verify that it is seated flush with the makeline.
  3. Verify that the dispenser is in its correct position. Refer to the CMG ingredient layout.
  4. This dispenser may be refilled on the makeline.

Cleaning and Sanitation

  1. Remove the dispenser from the makeline by grasping it on either end and lifting it up and out of the makeline.
  2. Remove Vertical Augers: Grasp the flat top of the augers and gently lift the auger off of the anchor point. Discard any excess food on the auger into a compost bin.
  3. Empty leftover food into a compost bin.
  4. Remove Horizontal Augers: Grasp the center of the auger shaft and gently remove the auger comes off of the anchor point. Discard any excess food on the auger into a compost bin.
  5. Remove and discard any remaining food product inside the dispenser into a compost bin.
  6. Remove the Slide Cover: Lay the dispenser on its front and press the tap on the inside of the dispenser body at the top of the slide. Once unclasped, slide the cover downward to release it from the dispenser body.
  7. Soak, scour, and scrub all pieces in a 3 comp sink to remove any large remaining food particles as necessary.
  8. Place all parts onto a dish rack, and run in a chemical sanitizing dishwasher for a full cycle.
  9. Remove parts from the dish rack, and load them onto a drying rack, allowing parts to air dry completely before the next use.
