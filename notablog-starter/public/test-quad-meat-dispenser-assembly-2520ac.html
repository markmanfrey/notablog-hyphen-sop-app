Quad Meat Dispenser

Parts

Hyphen Hint: While dispenser bodies may look similar, the chicken and steak dispenser bodies have a shorter slide to accommodate product specs.

Identification

This dispenser supports chicken and steak. The parts come in the following color(s).

Dispenser Assembly

  1. Gather the 6 components of the dispenser.
  2. Lay the dispenser down. Line up the top of the slide cover with the bottom of the dispenser slide. Gently slide the cover upward until the slide tab audibly clips into place on the dispenser body.

Install Horizontal Augers

  1. Horizontal auger 1 will be installed in the position closest to the dispenser slide. Insert the auger into the dispenser body until the base of the auger meets with anchor point 1. Slide the auger onto anchor point 1.
  2. Horizontal auger 2 will be installed in the position farthest from the slide. Insert the auger into the dispenser body until the base of the auger meets with anchor point 2. Slide the auger onto ancho point 2.

Install Vertical Augers

  1. Auger 3 will be installed in the position closest to the slide. Gently slide Auger 3 into the dispenser well and onto anchor point 3.
  2. Auger 4 will be installed in the position closest to the slide. Gently slide Auger 4 into the dispenser well and onto anchor point 4.
