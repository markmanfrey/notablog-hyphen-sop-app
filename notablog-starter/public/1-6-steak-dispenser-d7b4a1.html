1/6 Steak Dispenser

Description

This dispenser supports CMG Steak.

This dispenser has 5 parts:

Assembly

  1. Gather all the dispenser components
  2. Place the hopper on a stable surface.
  3. Insert the agitator into the anchor point inside of the hopper body. Place the base on the auger onto the auger point and gently turn until the auger slides into place. Repeat for the second agitator.

    Hyphen Hint: Both agitators should be facing the same direction when installed. This will allow them to move together instead of colliding when they begin to turn.

  4. Insert the augers into the anchor points inside of the hopper body in the dispensing area. Place the base on the auger onto the auger point and gently turn until the auger slides into place. Repeat for the second auger.
  5. The dispenser is ready for ingredient loading.

Fill Dispenser with Fajitas [Video]

  1. Line up a sixth pan of steak edge to edge with the side of the hopper and gently empty the contents into the dispenser.
  2. Hopper can be filled similarly on or off the makeline

Install Dispenser on the Makeline

  1. Install hopper on makeline with the front of the hopper facing towards you, grasp the hopper at the top of the left and right side and lift it up and into the makeline into the correct position. [Insert reference]
  2. Makelight guidance and/or incorrect installation warnings?
  3. This hopper may be refilled on the makeline.

Clean + San

  1. Remove the hopper from the makeline by grasping it on either end and lifting it up and out of the makeline.
  2. Discard any remaining food product into a compost bin, paying special attention to not discard any of the dispenser parts.
  3. Remove the augers from the dispensing area. Gently pull the auger up off of the anchor point. Repeat for second auger.
  4. Remove the agitators. Gently pull the agitator up off of the anchor point. Repeat for second agitator.
  5. Remove and discard any remaining food product inside the hopper.
  6. Soak, scour, and scrub all pieces in a 3 comp sink to remove any large remaining food particles as necessary.
  7. Place all parts onto a dish rack, and run in a chemical sanitizing dishwasher for a full cycle.
  8. Remove parts from the dish rack, and load them onto a drying rack, allowing parts to air dry completely before the next use.
