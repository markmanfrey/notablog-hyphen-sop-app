Quad Meat Dispenser

Parts

Hyphen Hint: While dispenser bodies may look similar, the chicken and steak dispenser bodies have a shorter slide to accommodate product specs.

Identification

This dispenser supports chicken and steak. The parts come in the following color(s).

Dispenser Assembly

  1. Gather the 6 components of the dispenser.
  2. Lay the dispenser down. Line up the top of the slide cover with the bottom of the dispenser slide. Gently slide the cover upward until the slide tab audibly clips into place on the dispenser body.

Install Horizontal Augers

  1. Horizontal auger 1 will be installed in the position closest to the dispenser slide. Insert the auger into the dispenser body until the base of the auger meets with anchor point 1. Slide the auger onto anchor point 1.
  2. Horizontal auger 2 will be installed in the position farthest from the slide. Insert the auger into the dispenser body until the base of the auger meets with anchor point 2. Slide the auger onto ancho point 2.

Install Vertical Augers

  1. Auger 3 will be installed in the position closest to the slide. Gently slide Auger 3 into the dispenser well and onto anchor point 3.
  2. Auger 4 will be installed in the position closest to the slide. Gently slide Auger 4 into the dispenser well and onto anchor point 4.

Completed Assembly

Fill Quad Meat Dispenser

  1. With the front of the dispenser facing you, line up a third pan of chicken edge to edge with the side of the dispenser and gently empty contents into the dispenser, to the right of the interior wall. Use a solid spoon to direct the product from the pan into the dispenser.
  2. DO NOT load food into the auger well
  3. Dispenser can be filled similarly on or off the makeline.

Install Dispenser on the Makeline

Install dispenser on the makeline.

  1. With the front of the dispenser facing towards you, grasp the dispenser at the top of the left and right side and lift it up and into the makeline.
  2. Place the dispenser onto the gear and verify that it is seated flush with the makeline.
  3. Verify that the dispenser is in its correct position. Refer to the CMG ingredient layout.

Cleaning and Sanitation

  1. Remove the dispenser from the makeline by grasping it on either end and lifting it up and out of the makeline.
  2. Remove Vertical Augers: Grasp the flat top of the augers and gently lift the auger off of the anchor point. Discard any excess food on the auger into a compost bin.
  3. Empty left over food into a compost bin.
  4. Remove Horizontal Augers: Grasp the center of the auger shaft and gently remove the auger comes off of the anchor point. Discard any excess food on the auger into a compost bin.
  5. Remove and discard any remaining food product inside the dispenser into a compost bin.
  6. Remove the Slide Cover: Lay the hopper on its front and press the tap on the inside of the hopper body at the top of the slide. Once unclasped, slide the cover downward to release it from the dispenser body.
  7. Soak, scour, and scrub all pieces in a 3 comp sink to remove any large remaining food particles as necessary.
  8. Place all parts onto a dish rack, and run in a chemical sanitizing dishwasher for a full cycle.
  9. Remove parts from the dish rack, and load them onto a drying rack, allowing parts to air dry completely before the next use.
