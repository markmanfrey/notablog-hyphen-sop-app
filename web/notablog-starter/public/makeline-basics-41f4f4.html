Makeline Basics

Primary Parts

Cabinets

Denester

Holds a stack of bowls which are released one at a time for each entree.

Dispenser (Cold/Hot) Cabinets

Holds cold or hot ingredients respectively

Lift

Takes a completed entree and moves it upwards to counter height for consolidation.

Cabinet Identification

The Lift Screen shows entree progress along the makeline using the key shown below. “H” stands for Hot and “C” stands for Cold

User Interfaces

Lift Screen

Primary user interface that communicates entree and fault information, and facilitates lifting an entree to the counter and resolve faults.

Cabinet Screens

Displays temperature and fault information.

Sub-Systems

Dispensers

Dispenser Wells

Counter Lights

The Counter Light is a valuable tool when encountering faults on the Makeline. It can guide the operator to specific locations where faults have taken place.

Each segment of the ring can be illuminated independently or all at once depending on the needs in the moment. The segments can be of any color and can animate (ie blink or pulse) as well.

Cabinet Lights

The Cabinet Light appears at the front of each cabinet, just above the Cabinet Screen. Currently they are only active for the Lift and Denest cabinets

Thermal Shields

Improve temperature stability and efficiency

Fill Positioners

Automated baskets that cooperate to move the entree from the Denester to the Lift
